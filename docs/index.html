UnitMath Demo

Calculator

Keyboard shortcuts

  • <Enter>: equals
  • <Escape>: clear
  • + (followed by space): add
  • - (followed by space): subtract
  • * (followed by space): multiply
  • / (followed by space): divide
  • ^ (followed by space): exponent
  • to (sourrounded by spaces): convert to

Examples

  • 1.5 m to ft
  • 1.5 m + 2 ft
  • 8 ft * 14 inch
  • 110 km/hr * 30 min

Note

This calculator cannot parse complicated grammar. It is just to highlight some of the capabilities of the UnitMath library. For a fully-featured math library with a parser that supports unit conversion and arithmetic, try Math.js.
